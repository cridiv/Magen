   - Token has identifiable cultural archetype AND

   - High score triggers: >60% holders under 7 days old, tx velocity spike >2.0, top 10 holds >50%, mentions from <3 accounts

5. reasoning: Ground it in the specific data. If you say "account age skew", cite the percentage.

Output ONLY valid JSON. No other text, no markdown, no backticks. If any required field is missing or invalid, the upstream system will retry.
